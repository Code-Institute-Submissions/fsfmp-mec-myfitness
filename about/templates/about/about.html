Our core performance range has been batch-tested by Informed Sport. This means your safe to use all our performance products such as Isolate, Concentrate, Recovery, Pre-Workout, BCAAs, Creatine.

What's the difference between whey and casein protein?

In general, it can be said that whey is a protein that is absorbed quickly in the body, which theoretically means that whey is better suited immediately after exercise. Casein is a slower protein and therefore saturates a little better. The big difference is the consistency, whey is thin and fits perfectly as a shake. Casein has a thicker texture and works well to make pudding, porridge or bake with. The most important thing to keep in mind to get the maximum effect from your strength training is not this but your total protein intake throughout the day, if you choose whey or casein will play less of a role. Try it out and choose the one you like the most!
